Mo Mee Ba

Baby Floyd is the modern music band officially formed in 2018 by a group of young music enthusiasts.
They are 4 of them in the band who all are currently a student. Sangay Thinley, 20, is the lead vocalist.
Ujal Karki, 20, is the lead guitarist. Kamal Adhikari, 23, is the bassist and Ujjal Gurung, 21, is the drummer.
Mo Mee Ba is their third song from their first album 'love the era'.
